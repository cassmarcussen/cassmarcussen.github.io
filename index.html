Cassandra Marcussen

I am a Ph.D. student in theoretical computer science at Harvard University. I recently graduated from Columbia University, where I double-majored in Mathematics and Computer Science. My email is cmarcussen-at-g-dot-harvard-dot-edu.

Academic Interests

I am interested in theoretical computer science, probability theory, and combinatorics. In particular, I like to think about problems related to property testing, information theory, computational complexity, randomized algorithms, and stochastic processes.
